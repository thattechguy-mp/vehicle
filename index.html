Vehicle, Trailer, or Boat Storage Information Form

Use this form to provide the required ownership, identification, insurance, and supporting documents needed before a vehicle, trailer, or boat can be stored under the related TSSA Vehicle, Trailer, or Boat Self-Storage Rental Agreement.

This form is for lawful storage intake only. If you are not the registered owner, or if the required ownership and insurance documents do not support the rental, the rental cannot be completed and you must contact the office.
Why this information is required: Information collected on this form is requested to verify ownership or lawful authority to store the property, to document the identity and contact information of the renter, to support the related TSSA Vehicle, Trailer, or Boat Self-Storage Rental Agreement, and to reduce disputes involving title, registration, insurance, possession, and removal of the stored property. If the name on the supporting documents does not match the renter information provided, the rental cannot be completed until the discrepancy is resolved with the office.
